I'm originally from the Chicago suburbs (go Bears!), but I have lived all across the country pursuing graduate education, earning five master's degrees along the way. Beginning my career researching evolutionary game theory and modeling in formal philosophy, I am now working towards a master's in Data Science and a PhD in quantitative psychology, researching network analysis, dynamical systems and generative AI/LLMs, all within the context of intensive longitudinal data with a focus on clinical applications. My dissertation focuses on applying complex systems analysis to uncovering the early warning signs of psychosis. I plan on graduating in Spring 2026.
